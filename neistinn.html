Instructions

Neistinn (in English ‘the spark’) trains learners of Icelandic to build simple sentences composed of subject, main verb and object. The app generates a sentence in English that has to be translated into Icelandic. The list of the vocabulary used in this application can be consulted by clicking on Vocabulary.

To start, click on the button Generate a sentence. A randomized subject, main verb and object will appear. The user can also ask the generator to add an adjective to the subject or the object when the button +adj appears. At that point, the user can type in the text boxes the correct Icelandic translation of the subject, the verb and the object and click on Verify to check whether the answer given is correct.

The items correctly translated will be marked in green and the incorrect ones in red. If no answer is given, the items will be marked in blue. The examples previously generated will be displayed on the side panel. The previous examples where the translation into Icelandic was incorrect are marked in red.
